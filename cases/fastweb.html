Telecom Enterprise UX Portal Design

Fastweb: Grandi Aziende

Designing the enterprise service portal for Italy’s leading telecom provider, streamlining complex service management for large‑business clients.

Role: Senior UX Designer
Client: Fastweb (Swisscom Group)
Year: 2018‑2019
Project Visual – Dashboard / Service Management UI

Challenge

Fastweb’s large‑business customers needed a unified digital platform to manage complex telecom services: fixed and mobile lines, cloud solutions, cybersecurity, and dedicated support. The existing portal was outdated, slow, and required multiple logins and manual processes.

Approach

I collaborated with product managers and engineers to redesign the entire enterprise portal. We introduced a modular dashboard, real‑time service monitoring, a ticket system integrated with Salesforce, and a clear pricing/contract overview. The design followed Fastweb’s brand guidelines while prioritizing usability for non‑technical business administrators.

Impact

The new portal reduced customer support calls by 40%, improved task completion rates by 55%, and became the standard for enterprise digital services within the Swisscom Group.

Tools & Methods

  • Figma, Adobe XD, Jira
  • Stakeholder Workshops & User Stories
  • Prototyping & Usability Testing
  • Integration with Salesforce API
  • Responsive Web Design
