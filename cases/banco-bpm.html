FinTech Mobile App Enterprise Design

Banco BPM Mobile Banking

Defining company‑wide mobile design standards for Italy’s third‑largest bank, impacting 4M+ users.

Role: Lead UX Designer
Client: Banco BPM
Year: 2020‑2022
Project Visual – Wireframes / UI Screens

Challenge

Banco BPM, Italy’s third‑largest banking group, needed a unified mobile experience across its multiple brands and user segments. Existing apps were fragmented, inconsistent, and failed to meet modern accessibility and usability standards.

Approach

I led a cross‑functional team of designers and researchers to define a scalable mobile design system. We conducted extensive user testing with retail and business customers, mapped complex banking journeys, and established a component library that supported both iOS and Android platforms.

Impact

The new design standards reduced development time by 30%, improved app store ratings from 3.2 to 4.5, and earned a LinkedIn recommendation from the client for “setting new benchmarks in enterprise mobile design.”

Tools & Methods

  • Figma, Sketch, Principle
  • User Interviews & Journey Mapping
  • Design System & Component Library
  • Accessibility Audits (WCAG 2.1)
  • Agile/Scrum with Dev Teams
